KPR College in Coimbatore refers to either KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology (KPRIET) or the newer KPR College of Arts Science and Research (KPRCAS), both part of the larger KPR Group (Textiles, Sugar, etc.), offering technical & arts/science programs focused on innovation, industry readiness, and holistic development with strong infrastructure, placements, and research. KPRIET (est. 2009) is an autonomous engineering college affiliated with Anna University, known for its research & engineering, while KPRCAS (est. 2019) offers Arts, Science, & Commerce degrees, training students to be job-ready citizens.
